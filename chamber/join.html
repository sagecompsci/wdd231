Non-Profit Membership Level

Non-Profit

Designed for registered non-profit organizations, this membership level offers community exposure and essential networking tools without the cost of for-profit tiers.

Benefits:

Inclusion in member directory

Access to networking events

Opportunity to participate in community outreach

Discounted booth space at chamber events

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze

Ideal for small businesses or startups looking to grow their presence and make local connections.

Benefits:

All non-profit benefits

Social media mentions and highlights

Member spotlight in monthly newsletter

Ribbon cutting ceremony for new businesses

Silver Membership Level

Silver

For established businesses ready to deepen community engagement and expand visibility.

Benefits:

All bronze benefits

Featured listing in business directory

Complimentary tickets to select events

Co-sponsorship opportunities for networking mixers

Gold Membership Level

Gold

Our premier level, Gold Members enjoy maximum visibility, influence, and recognition within the chamber and broader community.

Benefits

All silver benefits

Priority placement in publications and listings

Exclusive access to VIP networking events

Logo placement on chamber homepage

Opportunity to serve on advisory committees
