How do I join the server?

Joining the server is very simple. All you need to do fill out the whitelist application and join our discord server. You cannot play on the world unless you are a part of the discord server. You also MUST be whitelisted in order to be able to join the world.

How long will it take to get whitelisted?

It could take anywhere from a couple hours, to over a day. The server staff are not always available. It could take them some time to get your application. Rest assured, your application will be reviewed in due time.

Can I apply to be an admin or mod?

Of course! Once you have been whitelisted, please DM any mod or admin and let them know you would like to apply. There is a separate application for each. If you would like to apply to be both, you will have to fill out both applications.

Mod

Moderators help keep the server safe and fun for everyone. They handle rule enforcement, assist players, and report issues to admins. We're looking for friendly, fair, and active community members who know how to de-escalate and stay cool under pressure.

Admin

Admins are trusted leaders who manage major aspects of the server—everything from plugin setups to player disputes. They help shape the direction of the server and support both staff and players. Admin roles are typically offered to long-term, highly involved members with a strong track record.

Once you join the discord server, there are roles you can choose from. You can choose as many roles as you would like.
