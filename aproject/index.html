General

This is a 1.21.5 server, we will be upgrading to 1.22 once it releases and the required mods update.

This is a vanilla server. There are a couple of plug-ins, such as core inspect, multiple dragon egg drops, and custom map images.

You MUST fill out the whitelist application to be able to join the server. You can find it here.

Bedrock: bedrockhollowsmp.playit

Rules

Spawn is a safe area. No PVP.

Make teams, or play alone. It's totally up to you.

PVP is allowed, but be kind and have fun.

Griefing and stealing are not allowed unless both parties have agreed to have a war.

No duping, x-raying, cave finders, flying, seed finders, exploits, mob finders or any other kind of cheat.

NO HARASSMENT.
